Materials Management System Guidelines

New Purchasing Cycle

These are the proper steps to PR,PO and D/R in Materials Management System(MMS)

Purchase Requisition

The New Purchasing Cycle Starts here in Purchase requisiton module. Introducing our revamped purchasing cycle! Streamlined for efficiency and effectiveness, our new process aims to enhance your procurement experience. Discover simplified steps and enhanced features designed to optimize your purchasing journey from start to finish." You can open this site while making your transaction in Materials Management System.

Note: You can check the box to follow along while using both guide and MMS






Open Materials Management System

After you opened MMS, Click the Purchase Requisition from Transaction module:

Show filters and select the department you are in

Apply the filter

go to Step 2

Creation of Purchase Requisition

While on the PR module Click the new button to create PR:

A window popup will show the Purchase Requisition Details

There are fields that are needed to be filled out such as Requisition from and Requisition to

note: Input the neccessary details.

go to Step 3

Selection of items

To add the items in the request click the add button below on the bottom left of the window.

To select the items you must click the checkboxes and input the quantity.
note: you can search using the textfield above.

Input the right amount that you will requesting.

After you input the right item and quantity click select at the bottom right to continue.

go to Step 4

Posting The request

To Post the request click the request then click post at the top menu:

A confirmation window will appear

Click ok if you are sure of the details and items inside the PR.

Input your user code and password to confirm and post

Actual Process

Print Purchase Requisition Slip

Click the Print or preview to print.

Print Purchase Requisition Slip

Have it Signed by your Department Manager and Department Doctor

Note: In this Step it is not the President who will Approved your P.R it is your Head Doctor or Department Managers Once Signed by Manager and Head Doctor
