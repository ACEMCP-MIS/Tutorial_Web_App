How to Admit an ER/Outpatient Account?

      Note: To Admit a patient to Outpatient you must transfer the patient to Emergency

  • 1. Main Explorer>Outpatients
  • 2. Select and highlight a patient on the list
  • 3. Then click Transfer to ER under Sub Components.
  • 4. Go to Emergencies click the patient for Admission;
  • 5. Click the Admit Registry Account
  • 5. Input the details of the patient to the required fields
  • 6. Add the Doctor of the Patient from the Consultants . Physician tab
      • Note: Once done check the details again to avoid wrong entry.
  • 7. Save and close the form and then select the room that the patient will be assigned to.
